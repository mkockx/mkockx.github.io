Summary

Engineer and technology leader focused on the next generation of pharmaceutical manufacturing: AI enablement, industrial DataOps, smart manufacturing, and advanced analytics. I help manufacturing organizations turn fragmented systems and data into trustworthy, AI-ready foundations — defining the architecture, governance, and roadmaps that let data, intent, and analytics flow across the product lifecycle.
